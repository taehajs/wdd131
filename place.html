South Korea

Data

  • Population: 51.64 million (2023)
  • Capital: Seoul
  • Language: Korean
  • Currency: South Korean Won (KRW)
  • GDP: $1.8 trillion (2023 est.)
  • Major Industries: Electronics, automobiles, shipbuilding, petrochemicals, steel, textiles
  • Famous Landmarks: Gyeongbokgung Palace, N Seoul Tower, Bukchon Hanok Village, Jeju Island
  • Cultural Highlights: K-pop music, Korean cuisine, traditional festivals

Weather

South Korea experiences four distinct seasons. Winter is cold and dry, summer is hot and humid with monsoon rains, and spring/autumn are mild.

  • Winter (Dec-Feb): Cold, dry, occasional snowfall
  • Spring (Mar-May): Mild, blooming flowers
  • Summer (Jun-Aug): Hot, humid, monsoon rains
  • Autumn (Sep-Nov): Cool, crisp air, colorful foliage
