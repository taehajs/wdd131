Weather

South Korea experiences four distinct seasons: a cold, dry winter from December to February; a hot, humid summer from June to August; and mild spring and autumn seasons. The country receives most of its rainfall during the summer monsoon season.

  • Winter (Dec-Feb): Cold, dry, occasional snowfall
  • Spring (Mar-May): Mild temperatures, blooming flowers
  • Summer (Jun-Aug): Hot, humid, monsoon rains
  • Autumn (Sep-Nov): Cool, crisp air, colorful foliage
