Soccer World - Site Plan

Exploring the beauty of the world's most popular sport

Site Name

Soccer World

This name represents my passion for soccer and provides an informative hub about the sport rules, players, and experiences.

Optional domain availability: soccerworld-info.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an engaging hub for soccer fans. It will include information about the history of soccer, basic rules, positions, famous players, and my personal experiences as a young player.

Scenarios

  • What are the basic rules and positions in soccer?
  • Who are some of the most famous soccer players in history?
  • What strategies do teams use in modern soccer?

Color Schema

The following colors will be used throughout the site:

Navy Blue (#003366) – headings, header background
Gold (#ffcc00) – accents, buttons, highlights

Typography

Trebuchet MS – used for headings

Arial – used for body text
